CV

I am a masters student in the department of philosophy at Columbia University.

Most of my current attention is dedicated to metaethics, metaphysics, action, theory choice, and the philosophical issues of privacy (broadly construed). I have substantial, but more personal, interests in ancient greek and roman philosophy, latin literature, and translation theory.

I did my undergraduate work at the University of King's College and Dalhousie University, where I received a B.A. with First Class Honours in both Philosophy and Classics.

You can find my C.V. and contact information at the top right; I am always open for discussion, whether on the above topics, or on most anything else.

Spring of 2025, I co-edited an undergraduate philosophy journal, Corvus, which can be found on my links page.
