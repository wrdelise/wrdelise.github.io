I am a masters student in the department of philosophy at Columbia University. My current and primary interests fall under ethics (especially metaethics), metaphysics, epistemology, action, and the philosophical issues in privacy. I also have substantial interests in ancient greek and roman philosophy, latin literature, and translation theory.

I did my undergraduate work at the University of King's College and Dalhousie University, where I received a B.A. with First Class Honours in both Philosophy and Classics.

You can find my C.V. and contact information at the top right; I am always open for discussion, whether on these topics, or on most anything else.

Spring of 2025, I co-edited an undergraduate philosophy journal, Corvus, which can be found on my contact page.
